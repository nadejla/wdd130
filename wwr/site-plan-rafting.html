Audience

Who are the target customers?
At [Name Pending] Rafting Co., our primary target audience encompasses adventurous college students and dynamic young adults aged between 18 and 30, hailing from the vibrant communities surrounding our two local universities in Utah. Additionally, we cater to individuals and groups primarily aged between 25 and 45, with a moderate disposable income, who share a passion for thrilling outdoor experiences and seek a memorable escape from their routines. Our customers, including college students, are keen on exploring the beauty of nature through exciting river rafting adventures, finding not only an adrenaline rush but also a fun and invigorating break that aligns with their lifestyles.

What do they want and what needs of theirs aren't being met?
What they want is a dynamic outdoor adventure, and what they need is comprehensive guidance and equipment to make that adventure a reality. Many potential customers are unsure about what equipment is necessary for a successful rafting trip, and some may harbor reservations due to the fear of the unknown. Our website addresses these concerns by providing clear and detailed information on what to expect, how to prepare, and offers a seamless booking process.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Accessing our site primarily through mobile devices, our audience anticipates finding user-friendly information that not only answers their questions but also instills confidence in their decision to embark on a river rafting experience with us. By bridging the gap between uncertainty and excitement, our website aims to transform hesitant curiosity into eager anticipation, making every click the first step towards an unforgettable adventure.
